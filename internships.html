Full Stack Development

Participants will establish a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies, enabling the creation of fully functional web applications.

Learners will enhance their database management skills to perform efficient data operations across various database systems.

Students will design and implement RESTful APIs, ensuring seamless communication between client and server applications.

The course will prepare learners to deploy applications on cloud platforms, understanding essential hosting and scaling solutions.

By completion, participants will be equipped to handle real-world development challenges, producing high-quality, maintainable code.

Data Analytics

Participants will master crucial data analysis techniques utilizing statistical methods and analytics tools.

Learners will develop data visualization skills, effectively presenting insights through platforms like Tableau and Power BI.

Students will gain hands-on experience in processing and cleaning large datasets using SQL and other data management tools.

The course will explore predictive analytics, enabling learners to apply techniques like regression and classification to practical problems.

By the end of the course, participants will be capable of making informed, data-driven business decisions based on their analyses.
